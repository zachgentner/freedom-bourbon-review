Freedom Bourbon

700ml / 96 Proof / 48% ABV

A small-batch bourbon crafted by founder Ken Rusk to fuel the mission of the Comfort Peace Freedom Foundation. United we stand — in every pour.

Every bottle you buy helps give our veterans a fresh start: goal-setting resources, blue-collar career guidance, and a bridge from their pasts toward a future of stability and peace. Read Ken's message to you on the back of every bottle.

Blend

−

85% — 6 y/o Kentucky Straight Bourbon (75/13/12)

15% — 4 y/o Indiana High-Rye Bourbon (60/36/4)

Tasting Notes

−

Nose: Caramel, vanilla, toasted oak, dried corn and a touch of orchard fruit.

Palate: Big notes of brown sugar and baking spice balanced with dark cherry, leather and tobacco, finishing long with charred oak and honey.

Your Impact

−

From service to stability: proceeds from every bottle help a veteran set tangible goals, define their personal vision of the American dream, and achieve financial stability.

Your purchase puts life-changing resources — Ken Rusk's bestselling book, The Path course, and the Blueprint Sessions Workbook — into the hands of our veterans.
