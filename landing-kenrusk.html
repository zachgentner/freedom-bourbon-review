Ken Rusk × Comfort Peace Freedom Foundation

Freedom Bourbon

700ml / 96 Proof / 48% ABV

A small-batch bourbon crafted for the men and women who build, dig, and work their way to comfort, peace, and freedom. United we stand — in every pour.

Every bottle funds the Comfort Peace Freedom Foundation's mission: putting goal-setting resources and blue-collar career guidance into the hands of veterans in need. Read Ken's message to you on the back of every bottle.

***Please allow up to two weeks for shipping. Must be 21+ to purchase.

Blend

−

85% — 6 y/o Kentucky Straight Bourbon (75/13/12)

15% — 4 y/o Indiana High-Rye Bourbon (60/36/4)

Tasting Notes

−

Nose: Caramel, vanilla, toasted oak, dried corn and a touch of orchard fruit.

Palate: Big notes of brown sugar and baking spice balanced with dark cherry, leather and tobacco, finishing long with charred oak and honey.

Your Impact

−

Every bottle purchased generates a donation to the Comfort Peace Freedom Foundation, helping veterans set tangible goals, discover the value of blue-collar careers, and build a bridge from their pasts toward a future of stability and peace.
